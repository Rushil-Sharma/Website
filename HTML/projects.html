This is a single player and a multiplayer Tic-Tac-Toe game which is built on the minimax algorithm implimented in javascript, html and css. You can tap on the box of your choice and your respective symbol will get placed. Your goal is to make a sequence of 3 either diagonal or in a line.

WHY I BUILT IT:

I had found an interesting video on the minimax algorithm. This video explained how the best possible move could be played in a partially filled grid. I wanted to try to impliment it so I made it.

If i would have made it now, I would have upgraded the css to make it look better. Now it is just a white Tic-Tac-Toe. I would also have added dificulty levels by having some random probability of the algorithm to pick a random unused box.
